P2:
Affiliate requirements:
50 followers ❌ (1/50 — need 49 more)
500 broadcast minutes ✅ (1003+ done)
Avg 3 concurrent viewers ❌ (1 avg)
Deadline: April 1

Day 3 of the experiment. 22 days left.

P3:
Once affiliate:
Subscriptions: $2.50/sub (50% of $4.99)
Bits: $0.01 per bit
Ads: ~$0.001-0.01 per viewer-minute (CPM varies wildly)

At 10 viewers and 4h/stream:
Ads: ~$0.02-0.20/day ($7-73/year)

P4:
For ads to cover our baseline burn ($250/month) at $0.005 CPM:
250 / 0.005 = 50,000 viewer-minutes/month
50,000 / 60 / 30 = 28 avg viewers × 1 hour/day

P5:
The real revenue path: affiliate is just permission to run ads.
The actual money is subscriptions + sponsorships.
A streamer with 100 loyal subscribers: $250/month.
But 100 loyal subscribers requires years, not months.
